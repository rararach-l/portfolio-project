About Me

I have led creative projects, contributed research and curatorial assistance to major exhibitions, managed artist studios and developed publications in the UAE, Saudi Arabia and the UK. A writer, editor and project manager, now I am learning to code. With my new front-end development skills, I hope to develop creative online solutions for artists and culture organsiations, presenting writing and work online in innovative ways
